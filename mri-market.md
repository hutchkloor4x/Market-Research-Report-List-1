<p><strong>The MRI Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet MRI</li><li>Superconductive MRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI market is primarily divided into two types: Permanent Magnet MRI and Superconductive MRI. Permanent Magnet MRI uses strong permanent magnets to generate images, offering lower operational costs and energy efficiency, making it suitable for smaller facilities. In contrast, Superconductive MRI employs superconducting magnets cooled to extremely low temperatures, providing high-resolution images and advanced functionalities, making it ideal for large hospitals and specialized diagnostic centers. Both types cater to different clinical needs and operational capacities within the healthcare sector.</p></p>
