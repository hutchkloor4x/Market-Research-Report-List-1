<p><p>The Automatic Vehicle Fire Suppression System market comprises two primary types: wet systems and dry systems. Wet systems utilize water or foam solutions to immediately extinguish fires by cooling the flames and smothering them, making them effective for various vehicle types. Dry systems, on the other hand, deploy dry chemical agents to interrupt the combustion process, offering rapid fire suppression, particularly in high-temperature environments. Both systems are designed to enhance safety and mitigate fire risks in vehicles.</p></p>
