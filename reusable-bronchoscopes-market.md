Rigid bronchoscopes are solid, straight tubes that provide excellent visualization of large airways and facilitate interventions, making them ideal for surgeries and certain diagnostic procedures. Flexible bronchoscopes, on the other hand, are more versatile and maneuverable, allowing access to smaller airways and detailed examination, which is essential for various diagnostic and therapeutic applications in pulmonology. Both types are crucial for effective respiratory care.</p></p>
