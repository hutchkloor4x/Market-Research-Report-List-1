<p><strong>The Air & Oxygen Blenders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-flow Air/Oxygen Blender (0-3 lpm bleed flow）</li><li>Mid-flow Air/Oxygen Blender (3-6 lpm bleed flow）</li><li>High-flow Air/Oxygen Blender (6-12 lpm and Above）</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air & Oxygen Blenders market is categorized based on flow rates into three types: low-flow, mid-flow, and high-flow blenders. Low-flow blenders operate with a bleed flow of 0-3 liters per minute (lpm), making them suitable for neonatal or pediatric care. Mid-flow blenders, with a range of 3-6 lpm, cater to general adult patients requiring moderate oxygen therapy. High-flow blenders serve needs of 6-12 lpm and above, typically beneficial for critical care settings or patients needing intensive oxygen support.</p></p>
