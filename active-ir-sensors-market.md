<p><p>The Active Infrared (IR) Sensors market is primarily categorized into Thermal and Quantum Active IR Sensors. Thermal Active IR Sensors detect heat emitted by objects, enabling applications like motion detection and surveillance. They utilize thermopile or pyroelectric technologies. Quantum Active IR Sensors, on the other hand, leverage advanced semiconductor materials to detect infrared radiation with high sensitivity, often used in imaging and spectroscopy. Both types serve diverse sectors, including security, automotive, and industrial automation, driven by technological advancements and growing demand for accurate sensing solutions.</p></p>
