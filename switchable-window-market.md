<p><p>Switchable window technologies enhance energy efficiency and comfort by allowing users to control light, heat, and privacy. Electrochromic smart glass changes tint with electrical stimulation, providing dynamic shading. SPD (Suspended Particle Device) smart glass uses suspended particles that align to control transparency, offering quick adjustments. PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) smart glass switches between transparent and opaque states when electricity is applied, providing privacy on demand. Each type caters to specific applications in residential, commercial, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
