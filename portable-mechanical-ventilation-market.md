<p><p>The portable mechanical ventilation market encompasses two main types: non-invasive and invasive ventilation. Non-invasive ventilation provides respiratory support without the need for intubation, using masks or nasal prongs to deliver air, making it suitable for less severe respiratory conditions. Invasive ventilation involves the insertion of a tube into the airway, offering more intensive support for patients with critical respiratory failure. Both types cater to varying patient needs, emphasizing comfort and efficacy in diverse healthcare settings.</p></p>
