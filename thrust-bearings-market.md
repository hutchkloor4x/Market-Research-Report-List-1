<p><strong>The Thrust Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Thrust Bearings</li><li>Cylindrical Roller Thrust Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thrust bearings market is primarily categorized into two types: ball thrust bearings and cylindrical roller thrust bearings. Ball thrust bearings consist of balls positioned between races, allowing for smooth axial load handling and making them ideal for low-speed applications. In contrast, cylindrical roller thrust bearings feature rollers that provide higher load-carrying capacity and greater rigidity, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types play crucial roles in machinery and automotive sectors, ensuring effective load management and reliable performance.</p></p>
