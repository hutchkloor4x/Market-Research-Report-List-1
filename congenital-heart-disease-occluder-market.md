<p><p>The Congenital Heart Disease Occluder market focuses on devices used to treat specific heart defects. Patent Ductus Arteriosus (PDA) occluders address the abnormal connection between the aorta and pulmonary artery. Atrial Septal Defect (ASD) occluders close holes between the heart's upper chambers, improving blood flow efficiency. Ventricular Septal Defect (VSD) occluders target openings between the heart's lower chambers, reducing excess blood flow to the lungs. These devices enhance patient outcomes and are vital in pediatric cardiology interventions.</p></p>
