<p><strong>The EMG Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Biosensors</li><li>Intramuscular Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EMG biosensors market is divided into two main types: surface biosensors and intramuscular biosensors. Surface biosensors are non-invasive, utilizing electrodes placed on the skin to capture electrical signals from muscles, making them ideal for applications like rehabilitation and human-computer interfaces. In contrast, intramuscular biosensors are implanted within the muscle, providing more precise and detailed data on muscle activity, often used in clinical settings or for advanced prosthetics. Both types cater to diverse applications in healthcare, sports, and rehabilitation.</p></p>
