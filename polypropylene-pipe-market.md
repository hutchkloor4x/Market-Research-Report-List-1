<p><p>The polypropylene pipe market comprises three main types: PP-R/RCT, PP-H, and PP-B. PP-R/RCT pipes are known for their high thermal resistance and are commonly used in hot and cold water applications. PP-H pipes, characterized by their high pressure and temperature resistance, are often utilized in industrial settings. PP-B pipes offer excellent resistance to chemical exposure and are typically used in wastewater applications. Each type serves specific functions, catering to diverse industries and installation needs.</p></p>
