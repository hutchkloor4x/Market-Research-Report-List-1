<p><p>Flat panel and CRT displays are two primary types of display technologies. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) offers thin profiles and energy efficiency, using liquid crystals to produce images. LED (Light Emitting Diode) is essentially an advanced LCD that backlights the display with LEDs for better brightness and color contrast. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) provides superior image quality with deeper blacks and wider viewing angles, as it emits light per pixel. CRT (Cathode Ray Tube) displays, though outdated, utilize electron beams and glass tubes for image rendering.</p></p>
