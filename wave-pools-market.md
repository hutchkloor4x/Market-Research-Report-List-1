<p><p>Wave pools are categorized into two main types based on their water composition: fresh-water and salt-water wave pools. Fresh-water wave pools utilize treated pool water, making them ideal for family-oriented facilities and inland attractions. They offer a versatile surfing experience and are often easier to maintain. In contrast, salt-water wave pools replicate ocean conditions and provide a more authentic surfing experience. They are commonly found in coastal resorts or surf parks, appealing to enthusiasts seeking real ocean-like waves.</p></p>
