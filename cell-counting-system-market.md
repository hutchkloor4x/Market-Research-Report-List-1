<p><p>The cell counting system market encompasses various technologies, including spectrophotometers, flow cytometers, hematology analyzers, and cell counters. Spectrophotometers measure cell concentration by analyzing light absorption. Flow cytometers identify and count cells based on their scatter and fluorescence properties, enabling detailed analysis of cell populations. Hematology analyzers automate blood cell counting and classification, providing critical hematological data. Cell counters offer straightforward enumeration of cells, often utilizing manual or automated methods. Each type serves essential roles in research, clinical diagnostics, and biotechnology.</p></p>
