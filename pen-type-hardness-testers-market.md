<p><strong>The Pen Type Hardness Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Leeb Hardness Tester</li><li>Rockwell Hardness Tester</li><li>Brinell Hardness Tester</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pen type hardness testers analyze the hardness of materials through various methods. Leeb hardness testers utilize a rebound principle, measuring the impact and rebound velocity to assess hardness. Rockwell hardness testers gauge depth of indentation under a specific load, making them suitable for metals. Brinell hardness testers involve pressing a hard steel or carbide ball into the material and measuring the diameter of the indentation. Other types include Vickers and Knoop testers, each designed for specific applications and material types.</p></p>
