<p><ul><li>SMC</li><li>BMC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder In-Mould Coating (PIMC) involves applying a powder coating within a mould during the production of parts using materials like Sheet Moulding Compound (SMC) and Bulk Moulding Compound (BMC). SMC is a pre-impregnated composite that provides high-strength, lightweight components ideal for automotive and construction applications. BMC, a thicker version, allows for more intricate shapes and is often used in electrical and industrial applications. Both types benefit from enhanced durability and aesthetic finishes, resulting in improved product performance.</p></p>
