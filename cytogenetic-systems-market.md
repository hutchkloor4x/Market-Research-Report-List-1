<p><p>The cytogenetic systems market comprises various types of technologies used for genetic analysis and diagnostics. Fluorescence in Situ Hybridization (FISH) allows for the visualization of specific DNA sequences in cells, aiding in disease diagnosis. Comparative Genomic Hybridization (CGH) detects chromosomal abnormalities and copy number variations. Flow Cytometry enables the analysis of cell characteristics at a rapid pace, while Multiplex-FISH allows simultaneous analysis of multiple targets, enhancing diagnostic precision and efficiency in cytogenetic studies.</p></p>
