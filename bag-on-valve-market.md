<p><p>The Bag on Valve (BOV) market consists of three primary types: Aerosol BOV, Standard BOV, and Non-Spray/Low Pressure BOV. Aerosol BOV utilizes an aerosol propellant to deliver products in a fine mist, making it ideal for cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. Standard BOV is designed for general applications, providing a consistent spray without contamination risk. Non-Spray/Low Pressure BOV is suited for products that require controlled dispensing, such as creams or gels, focusing on precise application with minimal pressure.</p></p>
