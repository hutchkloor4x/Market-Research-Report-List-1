<p><p>The Organi Ginger Oleoresin market encompasses various extraction methods, primarily solvent extraction and supercritical fluid extraction (SFE). Solvent extraction involves using organic solvents to dissolve and separate the oleoresin from ginger, yielding concentrated flavors and bioactive compounds. In contrast, SFE uses supercritical carbon dioxide, providing a cleaner extraction process that retains the ginger's natural aroma and potency without harmful residues. Both methods cater to the demand for high-quality ginger extracts in food, cosmetics, and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
