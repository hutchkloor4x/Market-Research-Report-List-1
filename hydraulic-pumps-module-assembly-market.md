<p><p>The Hydraulic Pumps Module Assembly market encompasses various types including cylinder, eccentric wheel, plunger, spring, suction valve, and discharge valve. Cylinder pumps utilize pistons to create hydraulic pressure, while eccentric wheel pumps generate flow through an off-center rotation. Plunger pumps use reciprocating plungers for high-pressure applications. Springs assist in maintaining valve positions, ensuring system stability. Suction valves control fluid intake, while discharge valves regulate flow release. Each type plays a crucial role in hydraulic systems, contributing to overall efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
