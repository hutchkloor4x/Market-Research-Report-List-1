<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Park Assist Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Range Measurement</li><li>Proximity Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasonic Park Assist Sensor market comprises two primary types: range measurement and proximity detection. Range measurement sensors evaluate the distance between the vehicle and obstacles, aiding drivers in parking maneuvers by providing precise distance information. Proximity detection sensors, on the other hand, utilize ultrasonic waves to identify objects in the vehicle's vicinity, enhancing safety by alerting drivers to nearby hazards. Together, these technologies improve parking efficiency and safety, making them essential components in modern vehicles.</p></p>
