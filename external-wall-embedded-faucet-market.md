<p><p>The external wall embedded faucet market is divided into two primary types: manual water discharge and sensor water discharge. Manual water discharge faucets require users to physically operate a handle or lever to release water, offering simplicity and control. In contrast, sensor water discharge faucets utilize infrared technology to detect user presence, enabling touch-free operation, which promotes hygiene and water conservation. These types cater to different consumer preferences and usage contexts, enhancing functionality in various settings like homes and commercial establishments.</p></p>
