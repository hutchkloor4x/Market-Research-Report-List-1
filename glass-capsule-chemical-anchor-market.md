<p><p>The Glass Capsule Chemical Anchor market encompasses two primary types: Hammer-in and Spin-in anchors. Hammer-in anchors are characterized by their installation via a simple hammering action, which drives the anchor into the substrate, allowing for quick deployment and strong bonding. In contrast, Spin-in anchors require a rotational motion for installation, enabling precise placement and enhanced grip in various materials. Both types are essential for ensuring secure fastening in construction and renovation projects, contributing to structural integrity and stability.</p></p>
