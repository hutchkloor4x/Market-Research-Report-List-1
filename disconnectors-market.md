<p><strong>The Disconnectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centre-break Disconnectors</li><li>Double-break Disconnectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disconnectors are essential devices in electrical systems, used to isolate sections for maintenance and safety. Centre-break disconnectors feature a pivot point in the center, allowing both arms to swing outward for effective disconnection. They offer compact design and reliable operation in various applications. Double-break disconnectors, on the other hand, utilize two breaking points, resulting in reduced arcing and increased durability. This design helps in managing higher voltages and currents, making them suitable for heavy-duty operations in substations and industrial settings.</p></p>
