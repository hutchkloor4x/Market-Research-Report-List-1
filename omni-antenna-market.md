<p><p>The omni antenna market includes various types, primarily monopole and dipole antennas. Monopole antennas are typically vertical and offer 360-degree coverage, making them ideal for applications like mobile networks. Dipole antennas consist of two elements and provide balanced radiation patterns, often used in broadcast services. Other types include patch, loop, and whip antennas, each serving specific purposes in wireless communication. The selection of antenna type is influenced by factors like frequency range, gain, and application requirements.</p></p>
