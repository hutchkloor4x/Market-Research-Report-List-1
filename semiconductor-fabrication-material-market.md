<p><p>The semiconductor fabrication material market comprises various critical components. Silicon wafers serve as the substrate for device fabrication. Photomasks are used to project circuit patterns onto wafers. Photoresists are light-sensitive materials essential for defining circuit features. Wet chemicals facilitate cleaning and etching processes. CMP slurry and pads aid in chemical-mechanical polishing for surface finishing. Gases are utilized for various deposition and etching processes, while sputter targets provide material for thin-film deposition. Photoresist ancillaries support photoresist applications, and other materials encompass any additional components required in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
