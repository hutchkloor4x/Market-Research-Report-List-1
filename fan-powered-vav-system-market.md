<p><ul><li>Single-duct System</li><li>Dual-duct System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fan-powered VAV (Variable Air Volume) system market includes two main types: single-duct and dual-duct systems. </p><p>Single-duct systems utilize a single airflow path, where a fan assists in delivering tempered air into space, offering energy efficiency and temperature control. </p><p>Dual-duct systems combine two separate air streams—one hot and one cold—allowing for precise temperature management in diverse environments. Both types improve indoor air quality and adapt to varying occupancy levels, making them popular choices in commercial HVAC applications.</p></p>
