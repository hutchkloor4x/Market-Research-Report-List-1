<p><p>The Transcervical Tubal Sterilization market primarily includes two types: Tubal Ligation and Transcervical Implants. Tubal Ligation is a surgical procedure that permanently closes the fallopian tubes to prevent sperm from reaching the eggs. In contrast, Transcervical Implants involve the insertion of small devices into the fallopian tubes, inducing scarring that blocks fertility while being less invasive. Both methods serve as effective permanent contraception options, catering to different patient preferences and medical needs.</p></p>
