<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Climbing Ropes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Core Treatment</li><li>Dry Core and Sheath Treatment</li><li>Dry Sheath Treatment</li><li>Non-dry Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The climbing ropes market is segmented into four main types based on their treatment for moisture resistance. **Dry Core Treatment** involves applying water-repellent material only to the core fibers, enhancing strength while minimizing water absorption. **Dry Core and Sheath Treatment** applies hydrophobic treatment to both the core and sheath, providing maximum moisture protection. **Dry Sheath Treatment** focuses only on the outer sheath, offering some resistance while maintaining a strong core. Lastly, **Non-dry Treatment** ropes lack any moisture treatment, making them heavier and more susceptible to water absorption.</p></p>
