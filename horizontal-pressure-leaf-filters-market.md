<p><p>Horizontal Pressure Leaf Filters are used for solid-liquid separation in various industries. The market is segmented into two main types: Dry Cake Discharge and Wet Cake Discharge. Dry Cake Discharge filters remove moisture from the cake, resulting in a dryer, more compact solid that is easier to handle and transport. In contrast, Wet Cake Discharge filters retain some moisture within the cake, making the product more suitable for processes where residual liquid is required. Each type caters to specific operational needs.</p></p>
