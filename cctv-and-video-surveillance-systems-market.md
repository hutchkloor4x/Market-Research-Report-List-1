<p><p>CCTV and video surveillance systems can be categorized into two primary market types: wireless and wired systems. Wireless systems utilize Wi-Fi or cellular networks to transmit video footage, offering flexibility in installation and mobility. They are ideal for remote locations or areas with limited cabling options. In contrast, wired systems connect via coaxial or Ethernet cables, ensuring stable and high-quality video transmission with minimal interference. Wired systems are often preferred for their reliability in larger, more permanent installations.</p></p>
