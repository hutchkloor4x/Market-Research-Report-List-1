<p><p>The welding torches market is primarily divided into air-cooled and water-cooled types, each serving distinct applications. Air-cooled welding torches dissipate heat through ambient air, making them lightweight and portable, suitable for lower power applications. In contrast, water-cooled welding torches utilize circulating water to manage heat effectively, allowing for higher power output and prolonged use without overheating. Both types are essential in various industries, catering to specific welding needs based on efficiency, durability, and cooling requirements.</p></p>
