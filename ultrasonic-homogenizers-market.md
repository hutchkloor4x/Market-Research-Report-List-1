<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Homogenizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non Contact Type</li><li>Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic homogenizers are used to facilitate the mixing of substances at a microscopic level through high-frequency sound waves. The market is primarily segmented into two types: non-contact and contact types. Non-contact ultrasonic homogenizers employ ultrasonic waves that affect the sample indirectly, minimizing contamination and sample disruption. In contrast, contact-type homogenizers directly immerse a probe into the sample, allowing for intense mixing and shear forces. Both types serve diverse applications across industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and cosmetics.</p></p>
